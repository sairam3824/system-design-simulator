# Getting Started with System Design Simulator

## Overview

The System Design Simulator is an AI-powered platform designed to help software engineers practice and improve their system design interview skills. It simulates real FAANG-style system design interviews with an AI interviewer.

## Key Features

1. **AI-Powered Interviews**: Practice with an AI interviewer that adapts to your skill level
2. **Personalized Questions**: Questions tailored based on your profile, resume, and past performance
3. **Real-Time Scoring**: Get scored across 6 FAANG evaluation dimensions
4. **Performance Analytics**: Track your progress with detailed charts and insights
5. **Coding Challenges**: Practice coding problems alongside system design
6. **Resume Analysis**: Upload your resume for ATS scoring and personalized interview topics
7. **Global Leaderboard**: Compare your performance with other users

## Creating an Account

1. Navigate to the application at `http://localhost:3000`
2. Click on "Register" to create a new account
3. Fill in your email, name, and password
4. Click "Create Account"

## Logging In

1. Go to the login page
2. Enter your registered email and password
3. Click "Sign In"

## Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you'll see your dashboard with:
- **Total Interviews**: Number of interviews you've started
- **Completed**: Number of interviews you've completed
- **Average Score**: Your average score across all completed interviews
- **Quick Start Topics**: Popular system design topics to practice
- **Recent Interviews**: Your latest practice sessions
- **Performance Analytics**: Charts showing your score progression, skills breakdown, and topic performance

## Starting Your First Interview

1. From the dashboard, click on a topic card (e.g., "Design Twitter", "Design Uber")
2. Or navigate to "Start Interview" in the sidebar
3. Choose a difficulty level (Easy, Medium, Hard)
4. Click "Start Interview"
5. The AI interviewer will begin the session

## Navigation

Use the sidebar to navigate between sections:
- **Dashboard**: Overview and analytics
- **Start Interview**: Begin a new interview session
- **Coding**: Practice coding challenges
- **Resume**: Upload and analyze your resume
- **Leaderboard**: View global rankings
- **History**: Review past interviews
- **Profile**: Manage your profile and settings
