# Performance Analytics

## Dashboard Analytics

The dashboard provides comprehensive analytics to track your progress and identify areas for improvement.

## Score Progression Chart

The line chart shows your overall score trend over time:
- **X-axis**: Interview dates
- **Y-axis**: Score (0-4 scale)
- **Pass line**: Horizontal line at 2.5 indicates passing threshold
- **Trend indicator**: Shows if you're improving, stable, or declining

### Interpreting the Chart
- Upward trend indicates consistent improvement
- Flat line suggests you've plateaued - try harder difficulties
- Downward trend may indicate burnout or need for review

## Skills Breakdown (Radar Chart)

The radar chart displays your performance across the 6 FAANG dimensions:

1. **Requirements**: How well you clarify requirements
2. **HLD (High-Level Design)**: Architecture quality
3. **Detailed**: Implementation depth
4. **Scalability**: Scaling knowledge
5. **Trade-offs**: Decision-making ability
6. **Communication**: Explanation clarity

### Reading the Radar
- Points closer to the outer edge (4) are strengths
- Points closer to the center (0) need improvement
- A balanced shape indicates well-rounded skills
- Irregular shapes show specific areas to focus on

## Topic Performance (Bar Chart)

Shows your average scores by interview topic:
- **Green bars** (3.0+): Strong performance
- **Blue bars** (2.5-3.0): Passing performance
- **Amber bars** (2.0-2.5): Needs improvement
- **Red bars** (<2.0): Significant focus needed

## Insights

Automatically generated observations about your performance:
- Overall performance level
- Pass rate statistics
- Trend analysis
- Identified strengths and weaknesses

## Recommendations

Personalized suggestions based on your data:
- Specific areas to study
- Practice frequency suggestions
- Difficulty level recommendations
- Focus areas for improvement

## Performance Categories

Based on your average score:
- **Excelling** (3.0+): Ready for senior-level interviews
- **Proficient** (2.5-3.0): Solid performance, room for growth
- **Developing** (2.0-2.5): Making progress, keep practicing
- **Struggling** (<2.0): Focus on fundamentals

## Tracking Progress Over Time

### Best Practices
1. Complete interviews consistently (2-3 per week)
2. Review your scores after each interview
3. Focus on improving weak dimensions
4. Try different topics to build breadth
5. Increase difficulty as you improve

### Setting Goals
- Short-term: Improve weakest dimension by 0.5 points
- Medium-term: Achieve consistent passing scores
- Long-term: Reach "Excelling" category

## Leaderboard

Compare your performance globally:
- **Rank**: Your position among all users
- **Time frames**: Weekly, monthly, all-time
- **Filters**: By topic and difficulty
- **Trend**: Whether you're moving up or down

### Climbing the Leaderboard
1. Consistency is key - regular practice improves rankings
2. Higher scores on harder difficulties count more
3. Pass rate affects your ranking
4. Recent performance is weighted more heavily
