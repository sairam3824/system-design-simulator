# Profile Management

## Accessing Your Profile

Navigate to "Profile" in the sidebar to manage your account settings and preferences.

## Profile Information

### Basic Information
- **Name**: Your display name shown on leaderboards
- **Email**: Used for login (cannot be changed)
- **Avatar**: Profile picture (optional)

### Professional Details
- **Years of Experience**: Affects difficulty recommendations
- **Target Role**: Your career goal (e.g., Senior Engineer, Staff Engineer)
- **Bio**: Brief description of your background

### Skills
Add your technical skills:
- Programming languages
- Frameworks and libraries
- Cloud platforms
- Databases
- Tools and technologies

### Target Companies
List companies you're preparing for:
- FAANG (Facebook, Amazon, Apple, Netflix, Google)
- Tier 2 tech companies
- Startups
- Specific company names

## How Profile Affects Your Experience

### Difficulty Adjustment
- More experience → harder questions
- Fewer years → more guidance
- Skills match → relevant topics

### Question Personalization
- Questions tailored to your level
- Topics related to target companies
- Focus on your skill gaps

### Analytics Comparison
- Compare with similar experience levels
- Benchmark against target company standards
- Track progress toward goals

## Updating Your Profile

1. Go to Profile page
2. Click "Edit Profile"
3. Update any fields
4. Click "Save Changes"

## Password Management

### Changing Your Password
1. Go to Profile > Security
2. Enter current password
3. Enter new password
4. Confirm new password
5. Click "Update Password"

### Password Requirements
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one number
- Special characters recommended

- Leaderboard visibility

## Deleting Your Account

To delete your account:
1. Go to Profile > Account
2. Click "Delete Account"
3. Confirm deletion
4. All data will be permanently removed

**Warning**: This action cannot be undone. All interviews, scores, and analytics will be lost.

## Troubleshooting Profile Issues

### Can't Update Profile
- Check internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser

### Profile Not Saving
- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Check for validation errors
- Try again in a few minutes

### Skills Not Showing
- Add skills one at a time
- Use common technology names
- Avoid special characters
